#### Reflection

This assignment provided an important foundation in using the surface energy balance equation to calculate the rates of heat transfer in a system in steady-state. The surface energy balance equation is important as it can isolate specific regions within a system, allowing for the simplification of more complex systems. The assignment was also a valuable introduction to the role of thermal insulation in engineering. I compared the convective heat loss of a water heater without thermal insulation to the conductive heat loss of a water heater with insulation, and this comparison demonstrated that insulation significantly reduces heat loss and therefore power usage. This understanding will be useful in optimizing the efficiency and operating cost of systems such as water heaters, buildings, and industrial equipment.
